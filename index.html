The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Dr. Muhammad Yunus, a distinguished social entrepreneur and Nobel Peace Prize laureate, founded Grameen Bank, introducing the revolutionary concept of microcredit to combat poverty. His groundbreaking idea provides small, collateral-free loans to the underprivileged, primarily women, enabling them to create businesses and improve their livelihoods. This approach has empowered millions and significantly impacted global efforts to reduce poverty. Yunus also promotes social business, where profits address societal issues rather than personal gain. His work continues to inspire sustainable development initiatives, leaving a lasting legacy on economic and social policies worldwide."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
